# AI Voiceover Scripts for Devas Dental Clinic Video Ads

## Overview

These voiceover scripts are designed for Instagram Reels (9:16 aspect ratio, 1080x1920px) with a professional, warm, and trustworthy tone.

**Recommended Voice Settings:**
- Voice: Female (warm, professional) or Male (authoritative yet friendly)
- Speed: 100-115% (natural pace for clarity)
- Emotion: Confident, reassuring, caring
- Accent: Neutral (clear pronunciation for all regions)

## Production Guidelines

### AI Voice Generator Recommendations

**For ElevenLabs, PlayHT, or similar services:**

1. **Voice Settings:**
   - Stability: 85-90% (smooth, consistent delivery)
   - Clarity: 85-95% (clear pronunciation of dental terms)
   - Style: Warm/Caring for patient comfort
   - Background WC: Light (barely audible white noise for natural feel)

2. **Post-Processing:**
   - Apply gentle compression (ratio 2:1, threshold -12dB)
   - Add slight air reverb for natural studio feel (1-2% wet/dry mix)
   - Ensure consistent loudness (-16 to -14 LUFS for IG)

### Scene Breakdown Template

Each script follows this structure:
- **Opening Hook (0-3s):** Grab attention with benefit statement
- **Social Proof (3-10s):** Feature testimonial or statistic
- **Service Highlight (10-16s):** Showcase offerings
- **CTA (16-25s):** Clear call-to-action

## Additional Assets Needed

### For Video Editors

1. **Background Music:**
   - Type: Soft, warm instrumental (piano, acoustic guitar)
   - BPM: 80-100 (matches natural speech pace)
   - Volume: -24 to -20 LUFS (voice remains clear)

2. **Sound Effects:**
   - Soft "ding" when stats appear (subtle notification)
   - Gentle whoosh for page transitions
   - Soft click for CTA buttons

3. **Visual Assets:**
   - Tooth icon animation (SVG with morph paths)
   - Gold particle effects for premium feel
   - Slow zoom on testimonial for intimacy

4. **Text Animations:**
   - Use easing functions: easeOutQuart for entrances
   - Duration: 300-500ms for individual elements

## FAQ for Content Creators

**Q: What's the ideal frame rate for Instagram Reels?**
A: 30fps is optimal. Higher rates won't show improvement on Instagram's compression.

**Q: Should I add subtitles?**
A: Yes, always. Enable subtitles in the app, but also include burned-in text for accessibility.

**Q: How long should the video be?**
A: 15-30 seconds performs best for ad conversions. Keep first 3 seconds hook-focused.

**Q: What colors should dominate?**
A: Use your brand colors: Gold (#c8a96e), Warm beige (#f5efe4), and Deep brown (#2c1810).
